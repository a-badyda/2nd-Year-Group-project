Help Page - Frequently Asked Questions


What is MonsterMash?

Monster Mash is the craziest wackiest game since pong, come play our game and we will reward you with riches beyond your wildest dreams!

What's the point of the game?

The point of the game is too smash your oppenent like an egg.


How to Play MonsterMash

First send us a cheque valued between $5 and $90, then request an opponent to battle!!

Starting Out

You first start with a monster, you can then battle or breed with it.

Battling

To battle first invite all your friends to this game, then once you have friends ;-), you can go to the battle page and request to battle them!!

Breeding

You can breed with your friends' monsters, go to breeding page, you should see your friends monsters that you can breed with.

Aging

You monster starts ageing from when it is first born. Its strength and ability matures until a time when it decreases. It dies when its HP reaches 0.
