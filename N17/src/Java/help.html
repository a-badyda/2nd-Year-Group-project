Help Page


What is MonsterMash?

MonsterMash is a game where people can Battle, Breed, Buy/Sell or even Fight their monsters with friends.

Aim of the game?

The aim of the game is to battle and breed your monsters to gain ca$h and climb to the top of the scoreboard


How to Play

Sign up with a email and your off. Simply log in with that name and you will have a randomly generated monster to start off

Starting Out

When you first join you will be given a randomly generated monster. You can then add friends and battle, breed or even sell your monster. If you have no monsters do not worry a new randomly generated monster will be with you in no time.

Battling

To battle first invite all your friends to this game, then once you have friends, you can go to the battle page and request to battle them!!

Breeding

You can breed with your friends monsters, simply go to breeding page, you should be able to see your friends monsters that you can breed with.

Aging

You monster starts ageing from when it is first born. Its strength and ability matures until a time when it decreases. It dies when its HP reaches 0. So give it a life full of glory and win some fights.
